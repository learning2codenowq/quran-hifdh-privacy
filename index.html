Quran Hifdh

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 26th September 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Quran Hifdh ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Your privacy is sacred to us, and we handle your data with the same care we would want for our own Quran memorization journey.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Your name (optional, provided during onboarding for personalization)
  • Daily memorization goals and preferences
  • App settings and configurations

2.2 Memorization Data

  • Which ayahs you have memorized and when
  • Daily progress and statistics
  • Achievement milestones and badges earned
  • Revision schedules and completion data
  • Audio playback preferences and history

2.3 App Usage Information

  • Feature usage patterns within the app
  • Error logs and crash reports (anonymous)
  • Performance metrics for app optimization

Your Privacy is Our Priority: All your memorization data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your personal progress to external servers. Your Quran memorization journey remains completely private between you and Allah (SWT).

100% Local Data Storage

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • Track your memorization progress and calculate statistics
  • Personalize your Quran learning experience
  • Calculate achievements and unlock milestones
  • Optimize revision scheduling based on proven memorization methods
  • Improve app performance and fix technical issues
  • Provide technical support when you contact us

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All your memorization data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • We do not transmit your personal progress to external servers
  • Your data remains completely under your control
  • No third parties have access to your personal memorization data
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect local data
  • Your spiritual journey data is treated as an amanah (trust)

5. External Services and API Usage

5.1 Quran Content Delivery

Our app connects to external services to provide authentic Quran content:

  • Quran text from trusted Islamic sources with Uthmanic script
  • High-quality audio recitations from qualified reciters
  • Translation content from reputable Islamic scholars

5.2 Anonymous API Requests

Important: When fetching Quran content, no personal data is transmitted. API requests are completely anonymous and do not contain any user identification or memorization progress.

5.3 Network Activity

  • The app requires internet connection to download Quran content initially
  • Once downloaded, content can be accessed offline
  • Audio recitations are streamed and may be cached locally for better performance
  • No tracking cookies or advertising networks are used
  • External services may log anonymous request data for their own analytics

6. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and the safety of users
  • For technical support when you specifically request help

Zero Data Sharing: Your memorization progress, achievements, and personal settings are never shared with anyone without your explicit permission.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is designed to help Muslims of all ages memorize the Quran, including children. We take children's privacy seriously:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents should supervise their children's use of the app
  • All data is stored locally and not transmitted to external servers
  • The app does not contain any inappropriate content for young users

8. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access all your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data through app settings or by uninstalling the app
  • Export your data for backup purposes when transferring devices
  • Modify your settings and preferences at any time
  • Contact us with privacy concerns or questions
  • Request data deletion from our support records

9. Islamic Values and Data Handling

As a Muslim developer creating an app for the Muslim community, we handle your data according to Islamic principles:

  • Your memorization data is treated as an amanah (trust)
  • We do not use your data for any purposes contrary to Islamic values
  • The sacred text of the Quran is handled with utmost respect
  • We regularly make du'a for all users of this app
  • Transparency and honesty guide all our data practices

10. App Store Distribution

This app is distributed through Google Play Store and potentially other app stores. By downloading from these platforms, you also agree to their respective terms of service and privacy policies. We comply with all app store requirements for data handling and user privacy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new privacy policy in the app
  • Updating the "last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, providing additional notification through the app

Changes are effective immediately upon posting.
